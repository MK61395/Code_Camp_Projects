Timeline of Albus Dumbledore's Life

  • 1881: Albus Dumbledore is born in Mould-on-the-Wold
  • 1892: Receives invitation to study at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry
  • 1938: Becomes the Transfiguration professor at Hogwarts
  • 1945: Appointed as the Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry
  • 1956: Establishes the Order of the Phoenix
  • 1995: Leads the battle against Voldemort's return
  • 1997: Sacrifices his life in the Battle of Hogwarts
